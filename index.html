Build a Compost Bin

  • Tools
  • 1 empty 2 liter soda bottle
  • 1 pair of scissors
  • 1 small nail
  • 1 flat dish
  • 1 kitchen towel
  • 1 small pot
  • Compost
  • Small handful or dead leaves
  • Grass clippings or vegetable scraps
  • Shredded newspaper
  • Dirt from outside, not potting soil
  • 1 packet of seeds of your choice
  • 1 spray bottle filled with water

01/10

Clean the Bottle

Use soap and warm water to rinse the bottle and peel off the label. Then dry out the bottle with a paper towel.

02/10

Cut the Top

With the help of an adult cut off the top of the bottle, about 1-2‚Äù inches below the neck of the bottle. Set the top aside

03/10

Adult Only Add Air Holes

Use the nail to punch 8 to 10 small air and drainage holes along the sides and bottom of the bottle

04/10

Add the Starter

Put the bottle on the flat dish. Fill the bottle with the dirt and shredded newspaper. This will be the compost starter.

05/10

Wet the Starter

Fill the spray bottle with water and use to wet the compost starter

06/10

Add the Compost

Now add the grass clippings and vegetable scraps. These will be your compost!

07/10

Make a Funnel

Turn the top of the bottle upside down to rest in the opening. This will act as a funnel to add a little bit of water each day keeping the compost damp.

08/10

Give it Sunshine

Place your compost bin in a sunny spot then cover the top of your bin with a kitchen towel when not in use.

09/10

Watch it Work

Check the compost bin each day and see how it changes! As your compost breaks down you can add more vegetable scraps or fruit peels to it. Tip: You might see mold start to grow in the bin, this is normal, simply keep your bin covered when not working with it.

10/10

Start Planting Seeds

After a few weeks take some of your rich, healthy soil and place it in a small pot. Add some seeds and some water. Now watch it grow into a healthy plant. Subscribe to get plant care tips.
